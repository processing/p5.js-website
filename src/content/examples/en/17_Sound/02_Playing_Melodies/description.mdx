Many approaches to music include the use of scales and predetermined pitch sets. In western musical traditions pitches are given letter names like 'A' and 'C#.' There are many kinds of scales from all of over the world. This sketch demonstrates how one could play a 'D Minor Pentatonic' scale given the frequency values for the scale. It demonstrates how to use frequency values as well as note names.
