Sometimes analyzing the loudness of your sound input isn't enough. Sometimes we want to measure the instantaneous loudness of the individual components of our sound from, from low to high. In these circumstances we can use the p5.FFT() class, which provides a frame by frame analysis of loudness for individual 'frequency bands.' Using the FFT is used in most multi-band audio-visualizers.
