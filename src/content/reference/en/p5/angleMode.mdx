description: >
  <p>Changes the unit system used to measure angles.</p>

  <p>Degrees and radians are both units for measuring angles. There are 360˚ in

  one full rotation. A full rotation is 2 × π (about 6.28) radians.</p>

  <p>Functions such as <a href="./rotate">rotate()</a> and

  <a href="./sin">sin()</a> expect angles measured radians by default.

  Calling <code>angleMode(DEGREES)</code> switches to degrees. Calling

  <code>angleMode(RADIANS)</code> switches back to radians.</p>

  <p>Calling <code>angleMode()</code> with no arguments returns current angle
  mode, which

  is either <code>RADIANS</code> or <code>DEGREES</code>.</p>
