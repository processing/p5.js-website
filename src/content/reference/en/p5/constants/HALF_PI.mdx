  <p>A <code>Number</code> constant that's approximately 1.5708.</p>

  <p><code>HALF_PI</code> is half the value of the mathematical constant π. It's
  useful for

  many tasks that involve rotation and oscillation. For example, calling

  <code>rotate(HALF_PI)</code> rotates the coordinate system
  <code>HALF_PI</code> radians, which is

  a quarter turn (90˚).</p>

  <p>Note: <code>TWO_PI</code> radians equals 360˚, <code>PI</code> radians
  equals 180˚, <code>HALF_PI</code>

  radians equals 90˚, and <code>QUARTER_PI</code> radians equals 45˚.</p>
