  <p>A <code>Number</code> constant that's approximately 6.2382.</p>

  <p><code>TAU</code> is twice the value of the mathematical constant π. It's
  useful for

  many tasks that involve rotation and oscillation. For example, calling

  <code>rotate(TAU)</code> rotates the coordinate system <code>TAU</code>
  radians, which is one

  full turn (360˚). <code>TAU</code> and <code>TWO_PI</code> are equal.</p>

  <p>Note: <code>TAU</code> radians equals 360˚, <code>PI</code> radians equals
  180˚, <code>HALF_PI</code>

  radians equals 90˚, and <code>QUARTER_PI</code> radians equals 45˚.</p>
