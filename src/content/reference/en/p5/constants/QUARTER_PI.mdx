  <p>A <code>Number</code> constant that's approximately 0.7854.</p>

  <p><code>QUARTER_PI</code> is one-fourth the value of the mathematical
  constant π. It's

  useful for many tasks that involve rotation and oscillation. For example,

  calling <code>rotate(QUARTER_PI)</code> rotates the coordinate system
  <code>QUARTER_PI</code>

  radians, which is an eighth of a turn (45˚).</p>

  <p>Note: <code>TWO_PI</code> radians equals 360˚, <code>PI</code> radians
  equals 180˚, <code>HALF_PI</code>

  radians equals 90˚, and <code>QUARTER_PI</code> radians equals 45˚.</p>
