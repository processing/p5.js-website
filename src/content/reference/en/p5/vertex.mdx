description: >
  <p>Adds a vertex to a custom shape.</p>

  <p><code>vertex()</code> sets the coordinates of vertices drawn between the

  <a href="/reference/p5/beginShape/">beginShape()</a> and

  <a href="/reference/p5/endShape/">endShape()</a> functions.</p>

  <p>The first two parameters, <code>x</code> and <code>y</code>, set the x- and
  y-coordinates of the

  vertex.</p>

  <p>The third parameter, <code>z</code>, is optional. It sets the z-coordinate
  of the

  vertex in WebGL mode. By default, <code>z</code> is 0.</p>

  <p>The fourth and fifth parameters, <code>u</code> and <code>v</code>, are
  also optional. They set

  the u- and v-coordinates for the vertexâ€™s texture when used with

  <a href="/reference/p5/endShape/">endShape()</a>. By default, <code>u</code>
  and <code>v</code> are both 0.</p>
