  <p>Draws a circle.</p>

  <p>A circle is a round shape defined by the <code>x</code>, <code>y</code>,
  and <code>d</code> parameters.

  <code>x</code> and <code>y</code> set the location of its center.
  <code>d</code> sets its width and height (diameter).

  Every point on the circle's edge is the same distance, <code>0.5 * d</code>,
  from its center.

  <code>0.5 * d</code> (half the diameter) is the circle's radius.

  See <a href="/reference/p5/ellipseMode/">ellipseMode()</a> for other ways to
  set its position.</p>
