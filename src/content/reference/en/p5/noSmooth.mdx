description: >
  <p>Draws certain features with jagged (aliased) edges.</p>

  <p><a href="#/p5/smooth">smooth()</a> is active by default. In 2D mode,

  <code>noSmooth()</code> is helpful for scaling up images without blurring. The

  functions don't affect shapes or fonts.</p>

  <p>In WebGL mode, <code>noSmooth()</code> causes all shapes to be drawn with
  jagged

  (aliased) edges. The functions don't affect images or fonts.</p>
