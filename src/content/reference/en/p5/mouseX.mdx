description: >
  <p>A <code>Number</code> system variable that tracks the mouse's horizontal
  position.</p>

  <p><code>mouseX</code> keeps track of the mouse's position relative to the

  top-left corner of the canvas. For example, if the mouse is 50 pixels from

  the left edge of the canvas, then <code>mouseX</code> will be 50.</p>

  <p>If touch is used instead of the mouse, then <code>mouseX</code> will hold
  the

  x-coordinate of the most recent touch point.</p>
