  <p>Calculates the square of a number.</p>

  <p>Squaring a number means multiplying the number by itself. For example,

  <code>sq(3)</code> evaluates 3 × 3 which is 9. <code>sq(-3)</code> evaluates
  -3 × -3

  which is also 9. Multiplying two negative numbers produces a positive

  number. The value returned by <code>sq()</code> is always positive.</p>
