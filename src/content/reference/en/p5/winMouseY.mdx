  <p>A <code>Number</code> variable that tracks the mouse's vertical position
  within the

  browser.</p>

  <p><code>winMouseY</code> keeps track of the mouse's position relative to the
  top-left

  corner of the browser window. For example, if the mouse is 50 pixels from

  the top edge of the browser, then <code>winMouseY</code> will be 50.</p>

  <p>On a touchscreen device, <code>winMouseY</code> will hold the y-coordinate
  of the most

  recent touch point.</p>

  <p>Note: Use <a href="/reference/p5/mouseY/">mouseY</a> to track the mouseâ€™s
